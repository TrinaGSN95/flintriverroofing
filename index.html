What is the best roofing material? ▼

The best roofing material depends on your location, budget, and aesthetic preferences. Common options include asphalt shingles, metal, and clay tiles.

How often should a roof be inspected? ▼

It's recommended to inspect your roof at least once a year, or after major storms, to catch any damage early.

How long does a typical roof last? ▼

Most roofs last between 20 to 30 years, but this can vary based on materials and climate.

What are common signs of roof damage? ▼

Common signs include leaks, missing shingles, sagging areas, and granules in gutters.
